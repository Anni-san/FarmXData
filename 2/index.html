🌾 Rice Information

Complete Rice Insights

Essential information for farmers, retailers, wholesalers, and consumers.

👨‍🌾 For Farmers

  • 💰 Market Price: ₹1800 - ₹2200 per quintal
  • 🌱 Best Soil: Clayey & alluvial, good water retention
  • ☀️ Weather: Warm, humid climate (20–35°C)
  • 💧 Water Requirement: 1200–1500 mm (high water demand)
  • 🌾 Growing Season: Kharif (Jun–Nov) & Rabi in some states

🏪 For Retailers

  • 💰 Avg Retail Price: ₹35 – ₹60/kg (variety dependent)
  • ✅ Quality: Based on grain length, aroma & polish
  • 📦 Storage: Moisture-proof godowns
  • 📊 Demand: High, staple food in India

🚚 For Wholesalers

  • 📍 High Demand States: West Bengal, UP, Punjab, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh
  • 🌍 Distribution: Major export to Middle East, Africa & Europe
  • ⚖️ Bulk Deals: 50–200 ton lots common
  • 📦 Packaging: 25kg & 50kg poly/jute bags

👩‍👩‍👦 For Consumers

  • 🗓️ Harvest Time: 120–150 days from sowing
  • 📅 Shelf Life: 6–18 months (varies by storage)
  • ✅ Quality: Long grain (Basmati), Medium grain, Short grain
  • 🍚 Uses: Staple for meals, biryani, idli, dosa, snacks
  • 🥗 Health: Good carbs source, gluten-free, enriched varieties available
