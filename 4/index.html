🥭 Mango Information

Complete Mango Insights

Essential information for farmers, retailers, wholesalers, and consumers.

👨‍🌾 For Farmers

  • 💰 Market Price: ₹2500 – ₹5000 per quintal (variety dependent)
  • 🌱 Best Soil: Well-drained alluvial, pH 5.5–7.5
  • ☀️ Weather: Tropical & subtropical (24–30°C)
  • 💧 Water Requirement: 750–1200 mm annually
  • 🌾 Season: Flowering Jan–Mar, Harvest Apr–Jun

🏪 For Retailers

  • 💰 Avg Retail Price: ₹60 – ₹150/kg (variety & season)
  • ✅ Quality: Aroma, sweetness, size & skin color
  • 📦 Storage: Cold storage at 12–13°C
  • 📊 Demand: Peak in summer, processed demand year-round

🚚 For Wholesalers

  • 📍 Leading States: UP, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Maharashtra, Gujarat
  • 🌍 Distribution: Export to Middle East, USA & EU
  • ⚖️ Bulk Deals: 10–100 ton consignments
  • 📦 Packaging: Wooden crates, corrugated boxes

👩‍👩‍👦 For Consumers

  • 🗓️ Harvest Time: 100–150 days after flowering
  • 📅 Shelf Life: 7–14 days fresh, longer in cold storage
  • ✅ Popular Varieties: Alphonso, Dasheri, Kesar, Langra, Banganapalli
  • 🥭 Uses: Fresh fruit, juice, pickles, desserts, pulp industry
  • 💪 Health: Rich in Vitamin A, C & antioxidants
