🍅 Tomato Information

Complete Tomato Insights

Essential information for farmers, retailers, wholesalers, and consumers.

👨‍🌾 For Farmers

  • 💰 Market Price: ₹1500 – ₹2500 per quintal (highly fluctuates)
  • 🌱 Best Soil: Well-drained loamy soil, pH 6.0–7.5
  • ☀️ Weather: Warm climate (20–30°C)
  • 💧 Water Requirement: Moderate, 600–800 mm
  • 🌾 Growing Season: Kharif, Rabi & Summer

🏪 For Retailers

  • 💰 Avg Retail Price: ₹20 – ₹50/kg (seasonal variation)
  • ✅ Quality: Graded by size, color & firmness
  • 📦 Storage: Cold storage at 12–15°C
  • 📊 Demand: Consistently high

🚚 For Wholesalers

  • 📍 High Production States: Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, MP, Karnataka
  • 🌍 Distribution: Supplied across India & Gulf countries
  • ⚖️ Bulk Deals: 5–50 ton daily lots
  • 📦 Packaging: Plastic crates & gunny bags

👩‍👩‍👦 For Consumers

  • 🗓️ Harvest Time: 90–110 days after transplanting
  • 📅 Shelf Life: 7–15 days (depends on storage)
  • ✅ Quality: Lycopene content, firm skin
  • 🍲 Uses: Curry, salad, ketchup, soup, processed foods
  • 🥗 Health: Rich in Vitamin C, antioxidants & lycopene
