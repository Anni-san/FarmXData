🎃 Pumpkin Information

Complete Pumpkin Insights

Essential information for farmers, retailers, wholesalers, and consumers.

👨‍🌾 For Farmers

  • 💰 Market Price: ₹1000 – ₹3000 per quintal (variety dependent)
  • 🌱 Best Soil: Well-drained loamy soil, rich in organic matter, pH 6.0–7.5
  • ☀️ Weather: Warm climate, 20–30°C ideal
  • 💧 Water Requirement: Moderate, avoid waterlogging
  • 🌿 Growing Season: 90–120 days from sowing

🏪 For Retailers

  • 💰 Avg Retail Price: ₹30 – ₹80/kg
  • ✅ Quality: Firm, bright-colored, blemish-free pumpkins
  • 📦 Storage: Cool, dry place; lasts 2–3 months
  • 📊 Demand: High during festivals & seasonal markets

🚚 For Wholesalers

  • 📍 Major States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, West Bengal, Punjab
  • 🌍 Distribution: Domestic markets & food processing industries
  • ⚖️ Bulk Deals: 5–50 ton consignments
  • 📦 Packaging: Jute or mesh bags for protection

👩‍👩‍👦 For Consumers

  • 🗓️ Harvest Time: 3–4 months after sowing
  • 📅 Shelf Life: 2–3 months in cool storage
  • ✅ Quality: Smooth, firm skin, bright orange flesh
  • 🎃 Uses: Soups, pies, desserts, juices, roasted
  • 🥗 Health: Rich in fiber, vitamins A & C, antioxidants
