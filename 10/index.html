🍇 Grapes Information

Complete Grapes Insights

Essential information for farmers, retailers, wholesalers, and consumers.

👨‍🌾 For Farmers

  • 💰 Market Price: ₹5000 – ₹12000 per quintal (variety dependent)
  • 🌱 Best Soil: Well-drained sandy loam or alluvial, pH 6.0–7.0
  • ☀️ Weather: Warm, dry climate ideal; 15–30°C
  • 💧 Water Requirement: Moderate, drip irrigation recommended
  • 🌿 Growing Season: 90–150 days depending on variety

🏪 For Retailers

  • 💰 Avg Retail Price: ₹150 – ₹400/kg (variety & season dependent)
  • ✅ Quality: Firm, plump, uniform grapes without blemishes
  • 📦 Storage: Cool storage at 0–2°C, high humidity
  • 📊 Demand: High in urban markets, export quality preferred

🚚 For Wholesalers

  • 📍 Major States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Telangana, Tamil Nadu
  • 🌍 Distribution: Domestic consumption & export to Gulf, Europe, SE Asia
  • ⚖️ Bulk Deals: 5–20 ton consignments
  • 📦 Packaging: Plastic crates with cushioning, cold chain maintained

👩‍👩‍👦 For Consumers

  • 🗓️ Harvest Time: 80–120 days after flowering
  • 📅 Shelf Life: 1–2 weeks at room temperature, 2–4 weeks refrigerated
  • ✅ Quality: Firm, juicy, seedless or seeded as per variety
  • 🍇 Uses: Fresh eating, juice, wine, jams, desserts
  • 🥗 Health: Rich in antioxidants, vitamins C & K, improves heart health
