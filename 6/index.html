🍎 Apple Information

Complete Apple Insights

Essential information for farmers, retailers, wholesalers, and consumers.

👨‍🌾 For Farmers

  • 💰 Market Price: ₹3000 – ₹6000 per quintal (variety dependent)
  • 🌱 Best Soil: Well-drained loamy soil, pH 6.0–7.0
  • ☀️ Weather: Cold temperate climate, 21–24°C ideal
  • 💧 Water Requirement: Moderate, avoid waterlogging
  • 🌳 Growing Season: Temperate zones, harvested Aug–Oct

🏪 For Retailers

  • 💰 Avg Retail Price: ₹100 – ₹250/kg (season & type)
  • ✅ Quality: Based on size, color, sweetness & freshness
  • 📦 Storage: Cold storage for longer shelf life
  • 📊 Demand: Very high, premium fruit category

🚚 For Wholesalers

  • 📍 High Production States: Himachal Pradesh, J&K, Uttarakhand
  • 🌍 Distribution: Export to Gulf countries, Europe & SE Asia
  • ⚖️ Bulk Deals: 10–50 ton consignments common
  • 📦 Packaging: Wooden/plastic crates with cushioning

👩‍👩‍👦 For Consumers

  • 🗓️ Harvest Time: 150–180 days after flowering
  • 📅 Shelf Life: 2–4 weeks at room temp, 2–6 months in cold storage
  • ✅ Quality: Crisp, juicy, uniform-colored apples
  • 🍏 Uses: Direct eating, juices, desserts, jams, salads
  • 🥗 Health: Rich in fiber, antioxidants, vitamin C – “An apple a day keeps the doctor away”
